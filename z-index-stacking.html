Example 2: Position and apply a higher z-index to parent; children has a z-index of 0 or auto.

Browsers Tested: IE8/9/10, Firefox 22, Safari 6.0.5, Chrome 28.0.1500.95 (mac)

According to the spec, elements with a higher z-index are stacked in front of elements with a lower z-index. In this case, Box 1 has a higher z-index with a value of 3, Box 2 has a z-index with a value of 2, and Box 3 a z-index with a value of 1. Nothing new here!
